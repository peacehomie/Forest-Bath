The term 'Shinrin-Yoku' (which translates to 'forest bathing' in English) was coined by the Japanese Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry, and Fisheries back in 1982. It involves spending free time wandering in nature, and promotes wellbeing, health, and joy.

My idea with this project is to simulate part of that experience for you to enjoy at home, your office, or whereever else you might be. The real deal is hard to beat, but sometimes circumstances forces one to compromise.

If you'd be curious to learn more about me and the stuff that I do, feel free to check out my website.

Have a good one! /Felix
